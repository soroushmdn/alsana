About Us

AL SANA Petrochemical Trading L.L.C was established as an independent and active trading company with the aim of providing the best and utilizing the potentials of the Middle East region in the field of oil, gas and petrochemical products.


The main purpose of the holding is to sell oil and gas products, petrochemicals and minerals in international markets and to purchase equipment required by the oil, gas and petrochemical industry.

Outlook
